The Story

Carlos Yulo’s rise to prominence in the world of gymnastics is a story of hard work, dedication, and defying the odds. Born in Manila, Philippines, in 2000, Carlos was drawn to the sport at a young age, and his natural athleticism quickly caught the attention of coaches. Growing up in a country where gymnastics wasn’t traditionally a dominant sport, Carlos faced countless challenges—yet he never let those obstacles deter him. His relentless pursuit of excellence led him to train with top coaches and make the bold decision to move abroad to refine his skills.

Carlos Yulo’s breakthrough moment came in 2018, when he won the gold medal at the 2018 FIG World Artistic Gymnastics Championships in Doha, Qatar, becoming the first Filipino gymnast to ever win a World Championship title in gymnastics. This victory immediately put him on the map as one of the sport's rising stars. His performance in the men's floor exercise was nothing short of spectacular, showcasing his incredible agility, precision, and artistry.

His journey didn’t stop there—Carlos continued to push boundaries. In 2019, he won two gold medals at the 2019 Southeast Asian Games, cementing his status as a national hero. His performances have been marked by incredible consistency, and his mastery of difficult skills has set him apart from his peers. With every competition, he not only aimed for personal improvement but also represented his country on the global stage, inspiring others to pursue greatness in the sport.

Carlos' most significant achievement came at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics (held in 2021), where he won the silver medal in men's floor exercise, making history as the first Filipino gymnast to win an Olympic medal in gymnastics. The achievement was monumental, not just for Carlos, but for the entire country, as it showcased the world-class potential of Filipino athletes.

Beyond the mat, Carlos Yulo is known for his humility, discipline, and determination. His story is one of overcoming adversity and reaching for the stars, not just for personal glory but to inspire the next generation of Filipino athletes. Carlos has shown that no dream is too big when you have the passion, commitment, and perseverance to back it up.

From his early days in Manila to becoming one of the world’s most talented gymnasts, Carlos Yulo’s legacy is still being written, and the future is incredibly bright for this young champion.

Achievements

  • 2019 World Champion (Floor Exercise) – Won the gold medal in floor exercise at the 2019 FIG World Artistic Gymnastics Championships, making history as the first Filipino gymnast to win a World Championship title.
  • 2020 Tokyo Olympic Silver Medalist – Earned silver in men's floor exercise at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, marking the first-ever Olympic gymnastics medal for the Philippines.
  • 2× Southeast Asian Games Gold Medalist (2019) – Dominated the men's floor exercise and vault events at the 2019 Southeast Asian Games, winning two gold medals for the Philippines.
  • 2018 Asian Games Medalist – Won gold and silver medals in the 2018 Asian Games, showcasing his ability to compete at the highest levels in Asia.
  • Multiple Asian Championships Medals – Has earned several medals at the Asian Gymnastics Championships, further solidifying his status as one of the top gymnasts in the region.
  • First Filipino Gymnast in the Olympics (Men's Artistic Gymnastics) – Became the first Filipino male gymnast to qualify for the Olympics since 1968, paving the way for future gymnasts in the country.
  • 2017 Southeast Asian Games Gold Medalist – Claimed gold in floor exercise at the 2017 Southeast Asian Games, proving his dominance in the region.
  • Strong International Reputation – Recognized as one of the top gymnasts in the world for his skill, precision, and consistency in international competitions.
  • Breakthrough Victory at the 2018 World Championships – His gold medal at the 2018 World Championships made him a household name in gymnastics, earning him international acclaim.
  • Inspirational Role Model – Known for his determination, humility, and commitment to excellence, Carlos continues to inspire aspiring athletes in the Philippines and beyond.
